### AI usage guidelines





###### When should you use AI for assistance, and when should you rely on your own skills?

Use ai for assistance when need more information and when learning. Use actual skill for relevant work and sensitive data.

###### 

###### How can you avoid over-reliance on AI while still benefiting from it?

Only use ai to learn. Do not copy code/info over. Learn from it and implement it yourself. 

###### 

###### What steps will you take to ensure data privacy when using AI tools?

Do not input any passwords or sensitive information. If inputting data, use dummy data.
