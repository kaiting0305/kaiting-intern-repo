### Professional Reflection

###### Have you ever experienced or witnessed great teamwork and professionalism? What made it effective?

Yes. Splitting the work evenly, constant communication, leader check up in intervals, teammates double checking with each other. 

###### 

###### What steps can you take to make sure your communication is clear, respectful, and inclusive?

Treat others as how you would treat yourself. Address everyone with respect. Keep it professional.

###### 

###### How can you help create a positive, fun, and professional work environment?

Keep being respectful with everyone. Its okay to chat a bit. Makes atmosphere lighter and more enjoyable.

###### 

###### How can you prepare effectively for meetings with supervisors and colleagues?

Prepare beforehand. Know whats going to be discussed. Search up any terms or simple processes before the meeting.

###### 

###### What would it look like to be proactive during your internship?

Keep asking questions. Always looking for things-to-do and suggest it with supervisor. Then can start right away. Ask anything if you have any doubts.

###### 

###### How might you follow up with colleagues or supervisors if you need information/action from them? How would you escalate it further if you don't get a response and when would you escalate?

Email – for general comms (expect a reply within 48 hours)

Discord/Teams – for medium-priority stuff (need a reply within 24 hours)

SMS – urgent (need a response within 2 hours)

Call – true emergency



###### What are some things you won't do?

I won't treat people disrespectfully and will keep things professional.
